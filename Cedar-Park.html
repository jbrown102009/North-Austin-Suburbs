Welcome to Cedar Park

Cedar Park, Texas is a vibrant community situated on the northern border of Austin and just 17 miles from downtown.

~ Cedar Park, Texas

True to its namesake, Cedar Park now features 46 City-maintained parks within about 1,000 acres of City-owned parkland, along with 34 miles of trails.

Today’s population is about 83,1591. Cedar Park is the next major employment hub of the Austin MSA with a median age of 34.74 years – 51.21 percent with a Bachelor’s Degree or higher.
