Welcome to Pflugerville

Home to about 57,000 residents, this area is known for its beautiful trails, stunning lake, tons of shopping and restaurants, water parks and affordable homes. The city is super involved with its residents, too, hosting parades, festivals, and more each month.

~ Pflugerville, Texas

Pflugerville has experienced a tremendous rate of growth in population, since the turn of the millennium.

From 2010-20, Pflugerville's population grew 38.9%, from 46,936 to 65,191 people.
